User Flow

Step 1: The user enters their address in the form and then clicks “Register and verify my phone number.”
⬇️
Step 2: If the system detects that the phone number is linked to an active subscription, it displays a masked email and offers to send a login link or use another phone number.
⬇️
Step 3.1: (When the customer clicks “Send login link”), a one-time login link is sent to the masked email shown on their screen, allowing them to log back into their existing account.

If they can’t find the login link, ask them to check their Spam or Promotions folder or use the option to resend the link.
Step 3.2: (When the customer clicks “Use a different number”), the customer enters a new phone number, and an OTP is sent to that number to continue the checkout process.

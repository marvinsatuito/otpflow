New Phone Verification Sign-Up Process

Step 1
The customer enters their address and phone number in the form and then click the
“Register and verify my phone number”.
Step 2
If the system detects that the phone number is linked to an active subscription, it displays a masked email and offers two options:
Step 3
When the customer clicks “Send login link”, a login link is sent to the masked email shown on their screen, allowing them to log back into their existing account.

If they can’t find it, ask the customer to check their email's Spam or Promotions folder, or click the "Resend link" button to receive another login link via email.
Step 3
When the customer clicks “Use a different number”, they will enter a new phone number, receive an OTP for verification, and proceed with the checkout process.
